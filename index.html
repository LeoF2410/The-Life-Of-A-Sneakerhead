The Life Of A Sneakerhead

By Leodan Flores

Sneakerheads are probably the most passionate people over snkeares that you will ever meet, they wake up and get ready for new sneaker drops to happen. Crazy to think how these sneakerheads get their shoes and what they really do with these sneakers once they're in hand. Some people even go out of their way to wait in line overnight at their local retailers for a pair of sneakers.
